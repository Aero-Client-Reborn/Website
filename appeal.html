Aero Client Punishment FAQ


How do I appeal an Aero Client Punishment?

By creating a Punishment Appeal ticket in the Discord.

Can I purchase unbans?

No, you may not purchase unbans.

How do Aero Client Punishments work?

If you abuse the client's services, or are being overly toxic or are involved in anything illegal while on our client, you will be punished accordingly.

What are the Client's services?

The client's services consist of but are not limited to: the Launcher, Friends System, and Cosmetics System.

What do the reasons "toxicity" and "abuse" mean?

Toxicity means you've been banned or muted due to being toxic towards the community while using the client.

Abuse means that you've abused the client's services, or attempted to ruin another player's playing experience via the client's services.

I have questions that aren't listed here, where do I go to get answers to them?

You may join the Discord and ask your unlisted questions. If your question is reasonable enough we will add it here!
