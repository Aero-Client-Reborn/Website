How to install Aero Client on Mac

Since our Launcher does not currently have Mac Support, we've came up with a temporary solution!

The direct link to the client .zip: Click here


Follow these steps and at the end you will have Aero Client on your Mac!

Step 1: Unzip the .zip file

Step 2: Place the folder "AeroClient-1.7.10" thats in the .zip in ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/versions

Step 3: Open your Minecraft Launcher, click on the Installations Tab, make sure Modded versions is enabled. Example

Step 4: Click (+) New at the top, and set the Version to Release AeroClient-1.7.10, then click Create on the bottom right.
Example

Step 5: Click on the Play Tab and in the Versions list, scroll to the bottom, and click on the very bottom one, it should
say "AeroClient-1.7.10" below the name of the installation profile. Example

Step 6: Click Play!
