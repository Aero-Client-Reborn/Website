Frequently Asked Questions




What information does this website collect?

Nothing, we don't even track how many people download the client.

What is Aero Client?

Aero Client is a reskin of CheatBreaker, with way more features added!

Is it safe & free?

Yes! Aero Client is safe and completely free to download.

How do I get cosmetics on the client?

We have a shop via our Discord bot! You may redeem coins using $redeem every 5 hours and purchase cosmetics for coins using $shop and $buy! This does not require you to pay any real currency, only coins via our Discord bot!

How can I request for something to be added to Aero Client?

You may suggest new features in our Discord.

Can I use my own mods?

No, you may only use mods included in Aero Client.

Does Aero Client boost frames?

Aero Client will often more than double your frames in game.
