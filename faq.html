Frequently Asked Questions


What is Aero Client?

Aero Client is a modpack based from CheatBreaker, with way more features added!

Is it safe & free?

Yes! Aero Client is safe and completely free to download.

How do you get cosmetics on the client?

By being active in our Discord. In doing so you will level up, and the higher the level, the more rewards you'll get!

How can I request for something to be added to Aero Client?

You may suggest new features in our Discord by creating a ticket!

Why should I switch from another client to Aero Client?

Aero Client has more features than the mainstream clients, we offer a clean and less toxic playing environment.

Aero Client has some original features such as options for rounded module backgrounds, and even add things around the text!

Does Aero Client boost frames?

Aero Client will often more than double your frames in game!

How often does Aero Client get updated?

Aero Client is updated everyday, and there are hardly any bugs released in each update.
